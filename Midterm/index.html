The Villa

Welcome to the Villa

The Executive Center is a full-service conference facility specializing in training and seminars, providing the perfect environment for success. The Executive Center is located on Pepperdine University's Malibu campus nestled between the Pacific Ocean and the Santa Monica Mountains. With over 45,000 square feet of dedicated meeting space, the Executive Center provides state-of-the-art audio visual, exceptional food service, and an environment conducive to learning.

Conference Rooms

Each room is equipped with high-speed Internet access, ergonomic chairs, hard writing surfaces, electronic drop-down screens, built-in LCD projectors, and whiteboards with markers. The Executive Boardrooms offer 70-inch plasma screen monitors complete with fully-integrated and electronic whiteboards, printers, wireless high-speed Internet capabilities, and leather ergonomic boardroom chairs. All of the meeting space is situated just a few steps away from the Concierge Desk, on-site Copy Center and a Break Room that offers continuous refreshments throughout the day.
